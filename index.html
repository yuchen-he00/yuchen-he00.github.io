About Me

I am Yuchen He (和昱辰), a PHD student in the Basic Studies in Computing Science Lab (BASICS) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am supervised by Dr. Chihao Zhang.

I earned my bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2022.

Research Interests

My research area lies within theoretical computer science, specifically in sampling and online learning.
